Complete Tattoo Removal Solutions

Go Ink Away is Arizona's solution for clients who want full tattoo removal — whether the piece is small script or a full sleeve.

Complete removal requires breaking down every layer of ink deposited in the dermis. Go Ink Away uses Q-switched and picosecond laser technology to fragment ink particles so your body's immune system can flush them naturally. Each session progressively clears the tattoo, and Go Ink Away monitors your skin's healing between visits to adjust power settings, spot size, and wavelength for the safest, most effective results.

We solve:

  • Tattoo regret from names, dates, or symbols that no longer represent you
  • Faded but still-visible tattoos that cover-up artists cannot work over
  • Professional or career requirements that demand clear, ink-free skin
  • Tattoos with stubborn ink colors like green, blue, and white that other clinics struggled to treat
  • Scarring concerns from previous removal attempts performed elsewhere

Selective Tattoo Fading Solutions

Go Ink Away is the clinic cover-up artists in Arizona trust to fade existing tattoos to the right level before new work begins.

Not every client wants complete removal. Many need a tattoo lightened enough for a skilled tattoo artist to design fresh work over the old piece. Go Ink Away works directly with your cover-up artist to determine the ideal fade level, then delivers targeted sessions to reach that point — saving you time, sessions, and money. We stop treatment the moment your skin is ready for new ink.

We solve:

  • Multi-color pieces that need selective fading in specific areas only
  • Coordination between removal timeline and your tattoo artist's schedule
  • Over-treatment concerns — Go Ink Away fades to the level you need, not beyond it
